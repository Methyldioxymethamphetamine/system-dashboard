# DevCRM Pro — Functionalities

1. Scores leads from 0 to 100 based on organization size, usage patterns, and communication needs
2. Analyzes customer feedback and support notes to detect satisfaction or frustration levels
3. Predicts which customers are likely to leave up to 30 days before they churn
4. Automatically flags at-risk customer accounts and notifies the responsible account manager
5. Recommends the best time and approach for following up with a customer after a technical issue
6. Answers common customer queries about call quality, group chat performance, and platform features at any time of day
7. Transcribes sales and support calls and extracts action items into the CRM automatically
8. Summarizes long support threads into short, actionable notes
9. Tracks every customer interaction including calls, emails, and support tickets in one place
10. Imports leads via CSV file or web form and scores them immediately on entry
11. Moves leads through pipeline stages from initial contact to completed purchase
12. Segments customers by usage type such as video conferencing users, group chat users, and messaging users
13. Sends personalized outreach messages based on each customer's usage history and pain points
14. Re-engages dormant customers automatically based on their inactivity period and past issues
15. Identifies customers who are close to their plan limits and flags them as upsell opportunities
16. Generates visual reports on sentiment trends, churn probability, lead conversion rates, and call quality scores
17. Provides a management dashboard showing pipeline health, revenue, active leads, and average call latency
18. Allows admins to configure scoring weights for specific communication metrics
19. Backs up all data to the cloud in real time
20. Processes large AI workloads in the background without slowing down the main application
